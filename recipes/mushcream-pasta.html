Mushroom Cream Pasta

Description

Seared mushrooms cooked on dairy cream
with ham or turkey as an add.

    Ingredients

  • Pasta of own choice
  • Mushrooms
  • Dairy cream
  • Olive oil

  • optional:

    Ham / Turkey

    Steps

  1. Bring pasta to a boil
  2. Drizzle oil into the pan
  3. Chop up mushrooms into quarters
    while oil heats up
  4. Cook dairy cream and mushrooms
    at the same time
  5. Sear everything until bubbling occurs
  6. Combine directly with pasta or fractionate as desired
  7. Enjoy!
